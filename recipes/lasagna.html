Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a baked pasta dish, with meat, tomato sauce, and cheese. It's a delicious Italian dish that anyone could enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmeasan cheese
  • Egg
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Prepare the meat sauce
  3. Prepare the cheese mixture
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
  6. Let it rest
  7. Enjoy
