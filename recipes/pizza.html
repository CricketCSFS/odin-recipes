Pizza

Description

Pizza is one of the most loved foods. It's a flatbread dish with tomato sauce, cheese, and other toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Add the sauce
  3. Add the cheese
  4. Season the pizza
  5. Drizzle some oil
  6. Bake the pizza
  7. Enjoy
