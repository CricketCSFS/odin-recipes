Caesar Salad

Description

Caesar salad, one of the most classic salads, is a really simple salad, yet a delicious one.

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Caesar dressing
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Croutons

Steps

  1. Put the lettuce in a bowl
  2. Pour caesar dressing on the luttuce
  3. Add the croutons
  4. Mix the salad
  5. Sprinkle parmasean cheese over the salad
  6. Enjoy
